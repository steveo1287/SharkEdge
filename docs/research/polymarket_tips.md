# Polymarket Tips

Selective notes preserved from the legacy Polymarket screener materials.

## Practical research heuristics

- Watch for stale markets that have not moved with new information.
- Compare Polymarket prices to sharper external references before trusting a move.
- Check liquidity and volume together; sleepy markets can look more confident than they are.
- Pay extra attention to resolution rules and time to resolution.

## SharkEdge relevance

These tips are useful as research heuristics, not as direct product truth. If they become product signals, they should be converted into explicit measurable features and trap flags.
