# Polymarket Screener Notes

Restored from the surviving legacy Polymarket screener materials after cleanup.

## What it is good for

- listing active markets
- filtering by category or query
- finding longshot or extreme-price markets
- manual watchlist building
- basic public-API exploration without auth for reads

## Good SharkEdge uses

- manual research for cross-market divergence ideas
- sanity checks against sportsbook pricing narratives
- spotting interesting but non-core markets for future research queues

## Not for hot product paths

This is a research utility, not an app dependency. If any part becomes productized, it should feed a background job or offline report rather than runtime route logic.
